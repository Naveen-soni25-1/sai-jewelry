Our Story

Sai Jewelers is a family-run jewelry house in Sikar, Rajasthan, dedicated to the art of handcrafted ornaments.

We work in both gold and silver — heavy bridal and ceremonial gold jewelry made to order, and a ready collection of silver jewelry.

Proprietor: Mr. Ashok Kumar Soni, S/o Om Prakash Soni

What We Craft

From temple-style necklaces and kundan sets to lightweight daily-wear silver, every design is rooted in Rajasthani jewelry tradition.
